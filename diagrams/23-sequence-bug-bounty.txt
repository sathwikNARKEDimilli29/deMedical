sequenceDiagram

    participant Frontend
    participant Backend
    participant BugBountyContract
    participant UserRegistry
    
    Researcher->>Frontend: Register as researcher

    Frontend->>BugBountyContract: submitReport(severity, title, desc, hash)
    BugBountyContract->>BugBountyContract: Status: SUBMITTED
    BugBountyContract-->>Frontend: Report ID
    
    Note over Researcher,UserRegistry: Admin reviews
    
    actor Admin

    BugBountyContract->>BugBountyContract: Status: VALIDATED
    
    Admin->>BugBountyContract: approveReport(reportId)
    BugBountyContract->>BugBountyContract: Status: APPROVED
    
    Admin->>BugBountyContract: awardBounty(reportId) {value}
    BugBountyContract->>Researcher: Transfer reward
    BugBountyContract->>BugBountyContract: Update leaderboard
    BugBountyContract-->>Frontend: Bounty awarded
    
    Frontend->>Researcher: Reward received!
